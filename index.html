Why do people make origami?

Multiple studies in the modern day have shown that making origami has had positive results on stress in adolescents. This is my main reason for making origami because of how much work I've had lately. It has let me pour out my stress in a safe way by making calming pieces of folded paper. Another reason I want to make origami is because of how pretty you can make it by using patterned origami paper. Another reason why people might want to make/study origami is to get a look into the mindset of people in the past, because origami is a relatively recent discovery compared to other inventions within history.
